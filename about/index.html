About us

Accelerate. Innovate. Engineer.

SOCO Engineers is a global engineering technology company that accelerates innovation through advanced simulation, artificial intelligence, and secure cloud engineering.

Founded in 2011, with the vision to modernize how industries design, test, and deliver complex systems, SOCO Engineers merges deep engineering expertise with modern software and AI capabilities transforming traditional workflows into intelligent, connected ecosystems.

Our multidisciplinary team of engineers, data scientists, and software developers works at the intersection of physics, computation, and innovation enabling enterprises to achieve precision, speed, and security in every stage of product development.

From automotive and aerospace to energy, defense, and advanced manufacturing, we partner with global leaders and technology vendors to drive digital transformation and engineering excellence.

With operations across Europe, Asia-Pacific, and the Middle East, SOCO Engineers continues to redefine the future of simulation and engineering intelligence one breakthrough at a time.
